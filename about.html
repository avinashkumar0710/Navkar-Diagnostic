About Navkar Diagnostic

Excellence in Healthcare Services

Welcome to Navkar Diagnostic (Sonography Centre) and Eye Clinic, a premier healthcare facility dedicated to providing comprehensive diagnostic imaging and eye care services in Durg, Chhattisgarh.

Established with a mission to bring world-class healthcare to our community, we combine advanced medical technology with compassionate patient care. Our center offers a unique combination of advanced sonography services and expert eye care under one roof.

Located at the convenient Green Chowk location in Durg, we have become a trusted name for families seeking quality healthcare. Our team of experienced specialists is committed to delivering personalized care with the highest standards of medical excellence.
